1 WHAT WE'RE DOING

Bangar Valley is a stunning valley near Rudraprayag with almost zero tourists. We use content (reels, videos, posts) to make people fall in love with it online, so they visit, spend money, and that money goes to villagers — homestays, guides, food, workshops. Simple loop: content → attention → visits → village income.

2 WORDS WE USE

  • Reel — short vertical video on Instagram, our main weapon
  • Hook — the first 1.5 seconds of a reel; decides if people keep watching
  • Pillar — a content category; every post belongs to exactly one
  • Save / Share — the metrics the algorithm rewards most (more than likes)
  • CTA — "call to action": what we ask viewers to do (DM us, save this, visit)
  • Retargeting — showing ads to people who already watched our stuff
  • UGC — content our guests make; free and more trusted than ours

3 YOUR FIRST WEEK

  • Day 1: read the Mission + Pillars folders on this site
  • Day 2: watch the top 10 reels on @bangarvalleycollective, note why they work
  • Day 3: shadow a shoot day, learn the batch-shooting system
  • Day 4: draft 3 reel ideas mapped to pillars, get feedback
  • Day 5: ship your first reel + log it in your Team Output folder

4 THE ONE RULE

We don't promote a destination — we document a mission. Local faces are heroes, the story is honest, and every post must trigger a save, share or DM. If a post idea doesn't do one of those three, it doesn't get made. When stuck, ask BANGER AI (folder above) — it knows this whole playbook.

📁 strategy folder · 90 days to momentum

VIRAL MASTERPLAN

DAY 1–30

SEED — build the story engine

  • Lock cadence: 1 reel/day minimum, 5 stories/day, 2 carousels/week. Consistency beats perfection for 90 days straight.
  • Launch "Bangar Is Banger" as a numbered series (Ep. 1, Ep. 2…). Series train the algorithm AND the audience to come back.
  • Batch-shoot: 2 full shoot days/week producing 10–14 reels. A volunteer rotation owns editing so posting never stops when people leave.
  • Fix the profile funnel: bio = one line of promise + "Plan your visit ↓" link (WhatsApp or booking form). Highlights: Stay, Treks, How to Reach, Prices, People.
  • Geo-tag everything as Bangar Valley / Rudraprayag; create the location tag if weak. Post between 7–9 PM IST when Indian travel audiences scroll.
Goal: 30 reels live, 3 formats identified that beat the account average. In plain words: post every day, start the series, build the machine.
DAY 31–60

SPIKE — engineer the viral shots

  • Double down only on the 3 winning formats from Phase 1. Kill everything underperforming without mercy.
  • Creator hosting program: invite 4–6 micro travel creators (30K–300K, mountain niche) for free 2–3 day stays in exchange for 2 reels + collab post + story set. Barter, zero cash. Collab posts put BVC's handle in front of their entire audience.
  • Run the "nobody knows this exists" angle hard — mystery + FOMO is the highest-converting emotion for offbeat Indian destinations.
  • Start weekly long-form: one 8–15 min YouTube vlog/documentary episode. YouTube search is where "places near Chopta" planners actually convert.
  • Reply to every comment within 2 hours for the first 24 hours of each post — early engagement velocity decides distribution.
Goal: first 100K+ view reel, 2 creator collabs published, YouTube channel live. In plain words: repeat what works, borrow bigger audiences for free.
DAY 61–90

CONVERT — turn views into visits

  • Every viral reel gets a 24h follow-up story sequence: "You asked how to visit → here's the plan" with a WhatsApp link sticker.
  • Launch a bookable flagship: "Bangar Weekend" fixed-departure package (stay + trek + mud bath + theatre night, one price). People book packages, not vibes.
  • Post visitor UGC religiously — every guest's story gets reshared; ask every guest for one reel-worthy clip before they leave.
  • Turn on Meta ads (marketing folder) only now, once organic proof exists — ads amplify winners, they don't create them.
  • Monthly recap reel: "What we built in Bangar this month" — feeds the build-in-public loyalty loop.
Goal: first 20 paid bookings traceable to Instagram, WhatsApp community of 300+ intent-warm leads. In plain words: give the viewers a clear, easy way to actually come.

📁 marketing folder · paid + ground game

MARKETING ENGINE

RULE ZERO

Never ad-spend on unproven creative. Only boost reels that already beat the account's average organic reach. Ads scale winners; they can't save losers. Start ads in Phase 3 (Day 61+), not before.

Plain words: prove it works free before paying to spread it.
BUDGET

Start at ₹300–500/day total (₹9–15K/month). Split: 70% top-of-funnel reel boosts, 30% retargeting. Scale only what returns DMs/leads. This is homestay-affordable and enough to learn.

CAMPAIGN 1

Awareness — boosted banger reels. Objective: reach/engagement. Audience: 22–40, interests stacked (trekking + Himalayas + backpacking + travel pages), metros feeding Uttarakhand: Delhi NCR, Chandigarh, Dehradun, Jaipur, Lucknow, Mumbai, Bengaluru, Pune. Weekend travellers from Delhi NCR are the core market — the 8–10 hr drive radius converts first.

CAMPAIGN 2

Retargeting — the money layer. Audience: everyone who engaged with the page/reels in 90 days + profile visitors. Creative: the "Bangar Weekend" package with price, dates, faces of hosts. Objective: click-to-WhatsApp — Indians book travel in chat, not on forms.

CAMPAIGN 3

Lead form for groups. Instant forms targeting corporate HR / team-offsite planners and college trek clubs in Delhi NCR & Dehradun. One group booking = 15–30 heads = a village's good month.

CREATIVE

Use raw vertical reels as ads — polished ads underperform native-looking content. Always show a human + a price + one CTA. Test 3 creatives per campaign, kill 2 after 4 days.

MEASURE

Track cost-per-WhatsApp-conversation (target under ₹40) and cost-per-booking. Ask literally every guest "where did you find us?" and log it in a shared sheet — the cheapest attribution system that exists.

GROUND GAME

A GOOGLE LAYER

  • Google Business Profile for TNB Homestay + every experience — free, and it's how "places near Rudraprayag" searchers find you
  • Every guest asked for a Google review before checkout (target: 50 reviews in 90 days)
  • Add photos, pin the 7 Valley Trek trailhead, mud bath & Vasudev Mandir as Maps locations
  • Simple one-page site with itineraries so Google Search has something to rank

B OTA & LISTINGS

  • List TNB Homestay on Airbnb, MakeMyTrip Homestays, Booking.com — distribution you don't have to earn
  • Airbnb "Experiences" listing for the mud bath + trek combo
  • Tripoto & TripAdvisor destination entries + trip itineraries
  • IndiaHikes / trekking community forum presence for the 7 Valley Trek

C CORRIDOR CAPTURE

  • Bangar sits near the Chopta–Tungnath–Kedarnath tourist flow — intercept existing traffic instead of creating it from zero
  • Tie-ups with Rishikesh & Dehradun travel agents and hostel front desks (Zostel, GoStops) — commission per referred guest
  • QR-code posters at dhabas/cafés on the Rudraprayag route: "The valley nobody told you about → 40 min from here"
  • Local taxi drivers as referral partners — they're the real travel agents of Uttarakhand

D PRESS & COMMUNITY

  • Pitch the story (village rebuilding itself through tourism) to Curly Tales, LBB, Tripoto, Outlook Traveller, Better India — it's a ready-made feature
  • WhatsApp broadcast community: every DM asking "how to visit" gets invited; drip dates, offers, stories
  • College trek clubs (DU, Chandigarh, Dehradun) + corporate offsite planners: direct outreach with a group deck
  • Host one flagship event/season (theatre festival, harvest retreat) — events create press hooks and repeat visits

Principle: content creates desire, the ground game captures it. A viral reel with no Google presence, no WhatsApp funnel and no bookable package is footfall leaking out of a bucket.
